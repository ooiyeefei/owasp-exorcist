## Why These Tracks?

**Horror Ambience (Damned)**
- Creates immediate tension and unease
- Perfect for representing "corrupted" code
- Loops seamlessly without jarring transitions
- Not too loud or overwhelming

**Peaceful Garden (Sanctified)**
- Provides relief and calm after fixing vulnerabilities
- Represents "clean" and "secure" code
- Smooth, meditative quality
- Creates satisfying contrast with horror track
